This Years Catch

Throughout the year of 2020 I only fished in the waters of Indiana, therefore every fish from this gallery will originate from there as well. The main bodies I fished were the Kokomo Reservoir, Eagle Creek Reservoir, and other small ponds around my hometown (Kokomo, Indiana).

The lures I typically used were topwaters, craink baits, swim baits, and jigs. I mainly fished from my canoe or kayak, but sometimes would fish from the shore and from a boat.
